We are working to keep public institutions open to everyone. We need your help to ensure that everyone has access to education.

How you can help...

Join the protest!

Members of our team will be staying at the Garage until 9pm and will be back again Thursday and Friday from 9am-9pm. You are invited to join us at anytime when you are available!

Why is this policy immoral?

The criteria on which students are denied service in this instance does not fall into one of these categories and therefore is unjust discrimination.
